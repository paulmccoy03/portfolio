Python Backtest Equities • EMA

EMA Crossover Strategy on S&P 500 Constituents

Python • pandas • yfinance • Spyder

Built an EMA crossover backtest that downloads historical price data from Yahoo Finance, simulates trade entries/exits, and benchmarks against a buy-and-hold S&P 500 strategy.

  • Configurable short/long EMA windows and rebalancing rules.
  • Calculates CAGR, max drawdown, Sharpe ratio, and win rate.
  • Generates equity curve, drawdown chart, and trade log.

Time Series • VAR FX • Macro Shock

VAR Model for FX Spot Rates Under Tariff Shock

R or Python • VAR • FX

Estimated a vector autoregression on USD/EUR, USD/CNY, and USD/MXN spot rates, then simulated a tariff shock to study the impact on exchange rates over multiple quarters.

  • Includes data cleaning, stationarity checks, and lag selection.
  • Impulse response functions for a tariff shock scenario.
  • Visualizes projected depreciation paths vs. baseline.
